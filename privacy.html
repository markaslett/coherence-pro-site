Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. Here's exactly what we do and don't do with your data.

🔒 The Short Version

Coherence Pro does not collect, store, or share any personal information. All your data stays on your device. We don't have servers that store your information.

📱 Data We Collect

We collect no personal data. Specifically:

  • No account or registration required
  • No name, email, or contact information collected
  • No location data collected
  • No device identifiers collected
  • No usage analytics or tracking

💾 Data Stored on Your Device

Any data you create within Coherence Pro (such as session history or preferences) is stored locally on your iPhone using Apple's secure on-device storage. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

You can delete all app data at any time by removing the app from your device.

🏥 Apple Health

If you choose to connect Coherence Pro to Apple Health, data is read from and written to the Health app on your device only. We do not receive, transmit, or store any Health data on external servers.

You can revoke Health access at any time in your iPhone Settings → Privacy & Security → Health → Coherence Pro.

🍎 Apple Services

Coherence Pro is distributed through the Apple App Store and TestFlight. Apple may collect certain usage data in accordance with their own privacy policies. We do not control or have access to data collected by Apple.

Apple's privacy policy is available at apple.com/privacy.

👶 Children's Privacy

Coherence Pro is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children. Since we collect no personal data from anyone, there is no special risk to children's data.

✉️ Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or Coherence Pro, we'd love to hear from you.

Use our feedback page to get in touch, or email us at maslett@icloud.com.

📋 Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this privacy policy, we will update this page and revise the date below. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
